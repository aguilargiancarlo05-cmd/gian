My Autobiography

My name is Gian Carlo P. Aguilar, I prefer being called Gian. I am 15 years old, born on December 5, 2010, living in Munting Mapino, Naic, Cavite. For my academic background, I am a tenth-grade student at Cavite State University (CvSU) - Naic Campus. Growing up, I wasn't the extroverted type of person. I didn't have much friends, and mostly didn't interact with my relatives. But now, I have friends that I can trust with my life with. When I was a kid, I also had a passion for cooking. I got it when my Mom asked me to try and cook for the family. Once I did, I found the love for it. I would try to replicate recipes in the internet. Sometimes I cried because of failure, But I knew that I was working hard to practice more. Now, I dream of being a chef in Europe one day, hoping it'll make my family proud of me.
